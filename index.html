system design philosophy

The best systems are boring in production and interesting in architecture review. That is the target.

Make blast radius visible.

Isolation is not only an architecture diagram; it is a debugging interface.

principle.02

Migrations, rollbacks, onboarding, and incident response deserve product-level care.

Trust evidence over vibes.
